ELMO TEACHES KINDERGARTENERS ABOUT COMMUNISM

Written by Amon Gus

Resident of Sesame Street Elmo Monster spreads the word of Karl Marx at a local kindergarten. He first talked about the alphabet and how to count to ten. After he took a few drinks, he started a rant about the flaws of capitalism and the benefits of communism, and quotes Vladimir Lenin.

Some say this comes as no surprise, he has advocated for sharing in the past, as shown in this video. Cookie Monster went on FOX News to talk: "Me think he out of his mind. Me want Cookie. Me no want to share cookie. Dat communism."

Elmo has since campaigned to rename Sesame Street to the Union of Soviet Socialist Streets.
